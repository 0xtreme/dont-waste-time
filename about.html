About

Built to make time feel real again

Most life calculators stop at lifespan. This one keeps going. It subtracts the fixed routines that quietly absorb your days, then translates what remains into specific, emotionally legible moments: Friday evenings, dog walks, visits with parents, summers, full moons.

The point is not guilt. It is contact. If the numbers feel heavy, that means they managed to become concrete.

Design intent

The interface leans into quiet brutalism: restrained chrome, oversized serif numbers, warm near-black surfaces, and a slow visual rhythm. The hero number gets the space it deserves. Everything else arrives after it.

Privacy stance

This is a browser-first tool. Profile data stays in local storage on your device. There is no account system, no analytics dependency, and no server involved in the core experience.
