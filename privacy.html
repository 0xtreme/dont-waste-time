Privacy

Your data stays on your device

The MVP stores profile inputs only in your browser's local storage so return visits can reopen the dashboard instantly. The app does not require sign-in, does not transmit your profile to a backend, and does not depend on a remote database.

If you use the share export, the generated card is created locally in the browser. If you remove site data from the browser, the saved profile disappears with it.

This project is reflective software, not medical, legal, or financial advice.
